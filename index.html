Steps to Delete Your Account

If you want to permanently delete your account and all associated data from Top Club Fantasy, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to support@topclubfantasy.com with the subject line “Delete My Account”.
  2. In the email body, include the email address linked to your account for verification.
  3. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days of receiving your request.

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Your email address
  • Profile information
  • Usage and activity data

Retention Policy:

No data is retained after account deletion unless required by law (e.g., for billing or fraud prevention purposes).

Need Help?

If you face any issues, feel free to reach out to our support team at support@topclubfantasy.com.
